ABOUT

MISSION

We believe in a future where education is focused on helping people find their true passion.

Our games are here to let players have fun, learn by experimenting with different topics, and deep dive if they feel interested.

STORY

Luden.io was started as an R&D group at Nival, developers of Heroes of Might and Magic V.

  • In 2015 InMind VR and InCell VR were released. In 2016 InMind 2 was a launch title for Google Daydream. In 2017 VRobot was released for PSVR and ARrived became a launch title for Apple's ARKit. Awarded with App Store Best of 2017.

  • 2018: an educational game about machine learning while True: learn() was released for PC, consoles, and mobile.

  • 2019: Rewire was awarded with Epic Mega Grants.

  • 2021: an educational STEM game Learning Factory won the competition by Games4Change.

  • 2022: a game about space science and programming Craftomation 101 was launched.

More than 13 million players have installed our games on their devices.

GAMES

Learning Factory EDU

Learning Factory EDU is a free version of Learning Factory aligned with middle-school STEM NGSS Engineering Design Standards.

The pack of ten lessons introduces and allows kids to practice principles of the engineering design thinking process. During the course, learners will invent a Catisfaction Detector, a device capable of detecting cats' needs.

Backed by Games4Change as a winner of STEM Your Game Challenge.

while True: learn()

while True: learn() is a simulator of a machine learning specialist who uses visual programming to make his and his cat's living.

Everyone is speaking about machine learning. Forbes writes that these developers and Data Scientists have incredible salaries, Elon Musk says that they can accidentally create an AI that can enslave humanity. So, what are these people really doing when they come to work?

Learning Factory

Learning Factory is a factory automation game about machine learning and cats. Designed with help from educators who are using while True: learn().

The game is based on real machine learning tech which is a vital tool to succeed in the game. Our goal is to make Learning Factory a fun and gentle way to learn basic things of data analysis and machine learning.

Craftomation 101

A craft automation game about funny programmable robots CraftoMates who can move, eat, and craft things needed to terraform a frozen planet.

Players can use an in-game visual code system and learn programming and system design basics.

The game is free playable in web browsers and Chromebooks.

Teamwork Adventure

Teamwork Adventure is a tiny role-playing game that was co-designed with teachers, for teachers. It serves one simple purpose: to help spark communication and teach cooperation to students with autism and related disorders.

You can read a story of creation and how Leo Stern from Stanbridge Academy gave the game a shot.

Comes with a teacher guide.

Rewire

Rewire is a playful experience for teaching vital life skills, such as spatial orientation, shape recognition graphomotor skills, social interactions and more — all in form of fun mini games.

It was co-designed with certified specialists. They save 5+ hours a week by replacing piles of flashcards with Rewire.

You can read about Our Sunny World center experiment and Open Mind School experience.

Comes with a teacher guide. Works with PC and virtual reality headsets.

LEARN MORE

InMind VR 2

InMind 2 is an adventure game which places emphasis on the chemistry behind human emotion, greatly inspired by the Pixar/Disney movie "Inside Out" and (more scientifically) Lövheim’s theory of emotions. On the gameplay side, we take the best from our experiences developing the games InMind and InCell, to create an innovative and eye-catching VR experience.

The game was released Fall 2016.

VRobot

Something went wrong, robots and driverless cars flooded the entire planet. Humans built a Giant Robot to help them clean and reclaim their cities. Operate the Giant Robot. Use special tools like Tractor Beam, Transformers Sword, Lightning Hammer and Tornado Maker. Defeat SuperRobot 3000. Let people return to their homes.

The experimental game originally developed for the Epic Games Mega Jam 2016.

LEARN MORE

ARrived

ARrived is a 5 minutes long God simulation sandbox game for augmented reality inspired by Pocket God, Populous and Black & White classics.

The player assumes the role of a deity who must lead followers through key decisions and interacts with real environment in order to guide them reach prosperity and to all kinds of crazy stuff.

The very first game designed for Google's ARCore, Apple's ARkit and CoreML technologies. App Store Best of 2017.
