ABOUT

LET PEOPLE RETURN TO THEIR HOMES

If you have a Giant Robot you definitely have to use it to make the World a better place. Something went wrong, robots and driverless cars flooded the entire planet. Humans built a Giant Robot to help them clean and reclaim their cities.

The experimental project was originally developed for the Epic Games Mega Jam 2016. The game is based on controlling a Giant Robot to clean the planet of driverless cars and robots. Also, some of the roboprotectors like SuperRobot 3000 will try to resist but don't worry, they're just programmed to be like that.
