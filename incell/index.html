ABOUT

NEVER ENDING WAR AGAINST VIRUSES

InCell is an action/racing VR game with a bit of strategy and science thrown into the mix. You'll take an exciting journey inside the highly unusual micro world of human cell and stop the virus advance.

Getting closer to understanding cell structure and organelles functions (which are similar to the real functionality of the organelles) players will improve their skills and will have a chance to impress completely suspicious The Most Excellent Assistant Robot Zero-Seven.

This game is a perfect opportunity to test your VR device and learn more about cell microstructure, different organelles and their roles in cell vital activity.
